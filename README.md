# Sales Analysis Dashboard
Sales dashboard using pivot chart &amp; pivot table
This repository contains an interactive Sales Analysis dashboard designed to explore sales performance across years, product categories, regions, and customer locations.

## Overview

The dashboard provides a consolidated view of key sales metrics and visualizations, enabling quick insights into:

- Total revenue and profit performance
- Sales quantity and number of orders
- Discounts and their impact on profitability
- Category, subcategory, and product‑level trends
- Regional and state‑level customer distribution

## Key Metrics

At the top of the dashboard, several KPI cards summarize overall performance:

- **Total Sales**
- **Total Quantity**
- **Total Orders**
- **Total Profit**
- **Average Discount**

These indicators give an immediate snapshot of business health over the selected period.

## Visualizations

The dashboard includes multiple visual elements:

- **Category Analysis**
  - Pie charts for:
    - Total Profit by Category  
    - Total Sales by Category  
    - Total Quantity by Category  
  - Main categories: Furniture, Office Supplies, Technology.

- **Subcategory Performance**
  - Combined column and line chart showing:
    - Sales and quantity by subcategory (e.g., Chairs, Binders, Copiers, Phones).
  - Helps identify high‑revenue and high‑volume subcategories.

- **Geographical Analysis**
  - Filled map of the United States displaying:
    - Total customers (or sales) by state.
  - Useful for spotting strong and weak geographic markets.

- **Top Products**
  - Bar chart listing the **Top 10 Products by Sales**.
  - Highlights best‑selling items for promotion or inventory focus.

## Filters / Slicers

Interactive filters on the left side allow users to customize the view:

- **Year:** 2015, 2016, 2017, 2018  
- **Category:** Furniture, Office Supplies, Technology  
- **Quarter:** Qtr1, Qtr2, Qtr3, Qtr4  
- **Region:** Central, East, South, West  

These controls enable detailed drill‑downs, such as viewing a specific region in a single year or focusing on one category.

## Tools and Technologies

- **Data Visualization:** Excel 
- **Data Source:** Sales transaction data with fields for date, category, subcategory, product, region, state, customer, quantity, sales, discount, and profit.

## Use Cases

This dashboard can be used to:

- Monitor overall sales and profit trends.
- Identify top‑performing categories, subcategories, and products.
- Analyze the effect of discounts on revenue and profit.
- Detect regional opportunities and underperforming states.
- Support management reporting and data‑driven decision making.

## How to Open

1. Clone or download this repository.
2. Open the dashboard file:
   - For Excel: open the `.xlsx` file and enable macros (if applicable).
   - For Power BI: open the `.pbix` file in Power BI Desktop.
3. Use the slicers on the left panel to filter by year, category, quarter, and region.
